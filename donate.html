Donate Books

Have books to share? We accept new and gently used books for children and young adults (ages 0-18). Your books will find new homes with children who will treasure them!

What We Accept:

  • ✓ Picture books and board books
  • ✓ Chapter books and middle grade novels
  • ✓ Young adult fiction and non-fiction
  • ✓ Educational and activity books
  • ✓ Books in languages other than English

Please Note:

  • ✗ No books with torn pages or damaged covers
  • ✗ No textbooks or workbooks with writing
  • ✗ No adult books or inappropriate content
