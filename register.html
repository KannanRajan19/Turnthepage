Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I need to be to volunteer?

Volunteers aged 13 and up are welcome! Those under 18 may need parental consent for certain activities.

Is there a cost to participate in programs?

No! All of our programs are completely free for participants. We believe every child deserves access to literacy support regardless of financial situation.

How are Reading Buddies matched with children?

We carefully match volunteers with children based on availability, interests, and reading level to ensure the best possible learning experience.

What kind of training do volunteers receive?

All Reading Buddy volunteers receive training on effective reading strategies, working with children, and our program guidelines before being matched.
