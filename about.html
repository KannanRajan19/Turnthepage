About Us

Meet the passionate team behind Turn The Page Club and discover our story.

Our Story

Turn The Page Club began with a simple idea: every child deserves the chance to discover the magic of reading. Founded by three passionate high school students - Sahana, MJ, and Claire - our organization started as a small book drive in our local community.

What began as collecting a few dozen books quickly grew into something much bigger. We realized that donating books was just the beginning. Many children needed guidance and encouragement to develop a love for reading. That's when our Reading Buddies program was born.

Today, Turn The Page Club has donated hundreds of books, mentored dozens of young readers, and organized community events that bring families together through the shared joy of literacy. We're proof that young people can make a real difference in their communities.

Our Mission

To promote literacy and a love of reading among children in underserved communities by providing access to books, mentorship, and engaging educational programs.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to books and the support they need to become confident, lifelong readers who can unlock their full potential through literacy.
