Our Programs

Discover the many ways we're working to spread literacy and inspire a love of reading.

Book Donation Program

Our flagship program collects new and gently used books and distributes them to children who need them most. Every book has the power to open a new world of imagination and learning.

How It Works:

  • We accept donations of books for ages 0-18
  • Books are sorted, cleaned, and age-appropriately categorized
  • We partner with schools, shelters, and community centers for distribution
  • Each child receives books matched to their reading level and interests

Reading Buddies

Our mentorship program pairs trained volunteers with young readers for one-on-one reading sessions. Building confidence, improving skills, and fostering a lifelong love of reading.

Program Benefits:

  • Personalized attention and support for each child
  • Build reading confidence and comprehension skills
  • Positive role models who inspire a love of learning
  • Fun, engaging sessions tailored to each child's interests

For Volunteers:

  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Training and ongoing support provided
  • Make a meaningful impact in a child's life

Literacy Workshops

Interactive group sessions that make learning to read fun and engaging. Our workshops use games, activities, and storytelling to build foundational literacy skills.

Workshop Topics Include:

  • Phonics Fun: Learning letter sounds through games
  • Story Time Adventures: Interactive read-alouds
  • Word Building: Vocabulary expansion activities
  • Creative Writing: Encouraging self-expression through writing

Community Events

We bring families together through special events that celebrate reading and literacy. From book fairs to author visits, our events create memorable experiences around books.

Our Events Include:

  • Book Fairs: Free books for children to choose and keep
  • Story Time in the Park: Outdoor reading sessions
  • Reading Celebrations: Honoring reading achievements
  • Family Literacy Nights: Activities for the whole family
